## What is WandR?

WandR plans exploration trips without a predetermined destination. Just tell it what you're in the mood for:

> *"Find breweries, 2 hours, motorcycle, from Baltimore"*
> *"Scenic viewpoints, 3 hours, car, from Annapolis"*
> *"Romantic dinner spots, 1 hour, walking, from Bethesda"*

WandR's AI agents plan an optimized loop route with curated stops — then hands off to Apple Maps for navigation.

**Key features:**
- Natural language trip planning
- AI-optimized loop routes (always returns to your start)
- Native MapKit integration — no Google Maps dependency
- GPS auto-detection for your starting point
- Swipe-to-delete unwanted stops
